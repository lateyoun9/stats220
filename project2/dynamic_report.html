Tracking Online Advertising Patterns

This dynamic report summarises the advertising data collected through the Google Form and highlights the kinds of ads that appeared most often across different online platforms.

A total of 20 ads were logged in this dataset. The average ad duration was 18.9 seconds, and the longest ad lasted 35 seconds.

The ads were not equally persuasive. 9 of the logged ads were attractive enough to click on, which gives a click rate of 45%.

The most frequently observed platform was Instagram, with 11 observations. The most common ad type was Clothes, appearing 14 times.

The first bar chart compares how often ads were observed on each platform. This helps show where online advertising appeared most frequently in the data collection period.

Ad Types

The second bar chart focuses on the content of the ads. It shows which ad categories appeared most often and helps identify the main kinds of products or services being promoted.
